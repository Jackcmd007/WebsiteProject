About SNGCE Library

SNGCE has a central library and respective department libraries. The central library was founded in the year 2002 right from the inception of the college. The library is located on the second floor of the Administration Block. It consists of a Reference section, circulation counter, reading section, periodical section and Digital library. SNGCE Library has a vast collection of information resources - over 44552 Books (Print Copy) and 6006 eBooks (soft Copy) that includes technical, management, general volumes, ready references, encyclopedia etc. The library is also subscribing to national and international journals and e-Journal packages. SNGCE Library is fully automated with an open access system and a barcode based circulation process.

Objectives

  • To provide systematic and fast methods for the proper circulation of library books among the users.
  • To accommodate rare editions of trending technologies.
  • To update and procure the relevant and recent journals, and useful publications for faculty and student community.
  • To fulfil the basic needs to support teaching, learning and research.
